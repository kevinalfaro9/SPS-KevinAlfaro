List of Projects

  • Blueberry: This app was developed during my 7-week internship at Microsoft through the new Technologist Program in 2019. My team and I used Xcode/Swift in order develop the app. Essentially, the app was created to help procrastinators input their schedule along with upcoming tasks and we would use a sorting algorithm on the backend to prioritize the tasks that were most important and or due the soonest.
  • UniWire: UniWire was developed this past summer as a returning New Technologist intern with Microsoft. This time, my team and I implemented a web application using: React, HTML & CSS. The purpose of the web application was to connect college students who attend the same school in order for them to collaborate on whatever projects or ideas they had in mind. A user on the site could create a project or join an ongoing project they were interested in it. I was able to experience with project management, MVP, Agile, product life-cycle process and working with the HTML, CSS, and ReactJS.
